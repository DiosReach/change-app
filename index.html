What I Do

I specialize in capturing authentic, broadcast-quality sound on location — from intimate short films to major theatrical releases. Whether operating the boom or running the mixing cart, my focus is always on serving the story.

Background

Based in Taiwan, I built my foundation through intensive graduate film productions before expanding into television drama, theatrical features, commercials, and music videos. My credits include Raindance and Red Dot Award-recognized productions.

Approach

Great production sound is invisible — it pulls audiences into the story without calling attention to itself. I work closely with directors and cinematographers from pre-production onward, prioritizing reliability, adaptability, and calm precision on set.
